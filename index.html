Ahoy!

Welcome, glad to have you here! I'm Justin Leatherwood, a dude living in Wisconsin that likes programming and writing. I'm currently the Tech Lead at the IPM Institute of North America and a contract writer for the Ionic Framework blog.

I've long been intrigued by programming, ever since my first programming class at 14 when I built an amazing game in VB where a pixelated woman was launched from a cannon and superman had to catch her before she splatted on the ground. Okay, maybe it wasn't that amazing, but still. Programming intrigues me mucho.

Nowadays, I'm really into frontend web development, design and UX. While I really enjoy programming, I also love working with both technical and non-technical folks, often serving as the interface between the two worlds.

For more professional info, check out my LinkedIn profile -- I do my best to keep it up to date. For some publicly visible programming work, check out my GitHub account.
